A futuristic concept of sharing your feelings.

About the project

A loved one hurt you, you almost get hit by a careless driver when crossing the street, the doctor asks you about the nature of your pain. Don't you wish you could just show them how you feel?

This is an individual project from a course called Designing user experiences. The concept was visualized using Adobe XD software and had a timeframe of three weeks. The aim is to evoke emotions in the users, therefore I chose to work with emotion in emotions in its rawest form.

The idea

Empathy is a way for people to share their emotions and make others feel exactly the way you feel. The purpose of this is for people to understand each other in a way that words often cannot explain.
The goal of Empathy is to enable people to gain a deep emotional understanding of one another and see how their behavior affects others. It gives a multilayered emotional experience and can showcase a wide array of emotions and emotional combinations so that even the most complex emotions can be understood. Empathy has the potential to be used to make a difference in society, both on a private level and on a political level since people can finally start understanding one another.
