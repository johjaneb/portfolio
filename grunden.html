Developing an activity application for people with cognitive disabilities

This video tells the story of a maybe not so distant future, were a lonely human navigates the dull daily life on Mars. The earth has long ago been destroyed by pollution and climate change and when faced with the opportunity to return to earth, the protagonist faces a dilemma.

This project was a group effort in a course called Interactive storytelling and digital movie making. All graphics was drawn in Adobe Illustrator and later put to life using After effects.
