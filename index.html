About me

Hi there! I’m a UX and UI developer located in Göteborg. I love anything creative and that, in combination with a great interest in working with people, is how I ended up in this field. With my designs I strive to provide practical and easy to use designs that keeps the user intrigued by using visual interest. I find great interest in both graphical design and human computer interaction. I enjoy connecting with people and really like working close to the target group.

This portfolio has been created using HTML and CSS, with all design decisions based on UX and UI design principles from the book About face and other previous knowledge from my master studies. All pages of this portfolio follow a different theme, to show versatility and diversity in my design work.
